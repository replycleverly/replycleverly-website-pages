Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the ReplyCleverly Affiliate Program

Getting Started

How does the ReplyCleverly Affiliate Program work? ＋

The ReplyCleverly Affiliate Program lets you earn commissions by referring new customers to ReplyCleverly. You create custom Promo Codes from your My Code page and share them with your audience. When a customer signs up using your code, they get 10% off their first purchase and you earn a commission on their subscription for the first 12 months.

Who can join the affiliate program? ＋

The program is open to individuals and businesses including agencies, influencers, bloggers, consultants, media buyers, digital marketers, content creators, and more. You simply need to apply and be approved by the ReplyCleverly team.

How do I get approved as an affiliate? ＋

Submit your application through the affiliate signup page. Our team reviews each application and you will be notified by email once your account is approved. Once approved, you can log in to your affiliate dashboard and start creating and sharing your promo codes.

Commissions & Earnings

How much commission do I earn? ＋

You earn a commission on every paying customer you refer for the first 12 months of their subscription. Your personal commission rate is set by the ReplyCleverly team and is clearly displayed in the commission banner on your dashboard Overview page.

How long do I earn commission on a referral? ＋

You earn commission for the first 12 months of each referred customer's subscription. After 12 months, the commission period expires for that referral.

Do I earn commission on both monthly and annual plans? ＋

Yes. Commissions apply to both monthly and annual subscriptions. Your promo code gives customers 10% off their first purchase on either plan type.

When does a referral start counting for commission? ＋

A referral starts counting once the customer signs up for a paid subscription using your promo code. Free plan sign-ups do not generate commissions — commission tracking begins only when a customer subscribes to a paid monthly or annual plan.

Promo Codes

How does a Promo Code work? ＋

Your promo code is a unique discount code that customers enter at checkout when signing up for ReplyCleverly. They receive 10% off their first purchase and the referral is automatically attributed to your account. This applies to both monthly and annual plans.

How much discount do customers get with my Promo Code? ＋

Customers receive 10% off their first purchase when they apply your Promo Code at checkout. This discount applies to both monthly and annual plans.

Can I create multiple Promo Codes? ＋

Yes. You can create up to 10 custom promo codes from your My Code page. Each code must be unique across all affiliates, contain only letters, numbers, and hyphens, and be between 3 and 20 characters long.

Can I remove a Promo Code? ＋

Yes. You can remove any of your promo codes directly from the My Code page. Once removed, the code will no longer work at checkout. Any referrals already attributed to that code will remain in your account.

Payouts

When are commissions paid out? ＋

Commissions are only paid out after ReplyCleverly has successfully received the customer's payment and the minimum holding period has been met. This holding period exists to account for potential refunds, chargebacks, or payment disputes. Once both conditions are satisfied and your balance meets the minimum threshold, your payout will be processed.

How do I receive my payout? ＋

Payouts are processed via PayPal. You can add your PayPal account details from the Payouts page in your affiliate dashboard. Make sure your PayPal account is verified to receive payments.

Is there a minimum payout threshold? ＋

Yes. Your account must reach a minimum balance before a payout is initiated. Additionally, the payment from the referred customer must have been received by ReplyCleverly and the holding period must have passed. Details on the current threshold are available in your Payouts page.

What happens to my commissions if a customer cancels or requests a refund? ＋

Commissions are subject to a holding period to account for refunds and chargebacks. If a referred customer cancels or receives a refund within the holding period, the associated commission may be reversed. Cancelled referrals are clearly marked in your Referrals page.

Rules & Conduct

Can I use my own Promo Code for my own subscription? ＋

No. Self-referral is strictly prohibited. You may not use your own Promo Code on a personal subscription or share it with someone specifically to benefit yourself. Violations may result in account termination and forfeiture of earned commissions.

What marketing practices are not allowed? ＋

You may not send spam or unsolicited messages, misrepresent ReplyCleverly features or pricing, use deceptive marketing, impersonate the ReplyCleverly brand, offer additional incentives beyond the approved 10% customer discount, or share/sell your Promo Code to other affiliates.

Can ReplyCleverly terminate my affiliate account? ＋

Yes. ReplyCleverly may suspend or terminate your affiliate account if you violate the program terms, engage in fraudulent activity, or if the program is discontinued. Commissions earned in good faith prior to termination will be paid at the next scheduled payout if they meet the minimum threshold.

While the SOC 2 Type II report is a detailed confidential audit, the SOC 3 Type II report serves as a public-facing confirmation that the underlying technology meets recognised standards for security, availability, and data handling.
